Stat Formulas
Overall Weighting iA player's overall (OVR) is a single 50–99 rating. Each of the four stats is multiplied by its weight below and added into one score; that score is then ranked against every other player and mapped to 50–99 — so the top player is 99 and the lowest is 50. The specialist boost can nudge it up for lopsided players.weighted = Σ (stat × weight)
OVR = 50 + percentile(weighted) × 49
iIf a player's best stat is far above their others, the overall gets a small boost so specialists (e.g. a dominant defender) aren't undervalued by the weighted average. Threshold = points above the rest before it kicks in.gap = best − avg(other 3)
