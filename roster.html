Card Stats Explained

All stats are per-period averages — normalised so players with fewer games aren't unfairly penalised. Each stat is then percentile-ranked against the full player pool and scaled to 50–99.

What Each Stat Measures
SHOShootingGoals per period
ASTAssistsAssists per period
PASPassingPasses per period
DEFDefenceSaves & Blocks per period (saves weighted double)
Specialist Boost
If a player's top stat is 15+ points above their other stats, they receive an overall boost. This prevents highly specialised players (e.g. a dominant defender) from being undervalued by a weighted average.
10-Period Minimum
Players with fewer than 10 periods played have their rating calculated but capped at 69 (Silver max) to prevent small sample sizes inflating scores.
